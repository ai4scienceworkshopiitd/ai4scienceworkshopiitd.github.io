The Yardi School of Artificial Intelligence (ScAI) will host a one-day workshop, “AI for Science and Engineering”, at the Research and Innovation Park, Indian Institute of Technology Delhi on 12 February 2026. The event will bring together researchers from academia and industry to discuss advances at the intersection of artificial intelligence and scientific discovery, including applications in engineering and design, materials science, and environmental research.
